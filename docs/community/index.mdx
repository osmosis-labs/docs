# Community

Ways to contribute to Osmosis and participate in its ecosystem.

**Governance Participation** explains how to discuss, vote on, and submit proposals. **Grants and Ecosystem Funding** points to the funding paths for building on Osmosis, and **Support and Where to Get Help** is the directory of support channels. **Contributing** covers how to work with the Osmosis development repositories: issues, branching, and the contribution process. The **IBC Relayer List** is a directory of relaying operators connecting Osmosis to the wider Cosmos ecosystem.
