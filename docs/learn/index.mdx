# Learn Osmosis

Osmosis is an onchain decentralized exchange and the liquidity hub of the Cosmos ecosystem. It lets anyone trade, provide liquidity, and launch assets across dozens of interconnected chains, with the trading logic, liquidity, and governance all living onchain.

This section explains the ideas behind Osmosis: what the protocol is, how its markets and tokenomics work, and the concepts you will meet throughout the rest of the docs. No code or node setup here, just the mental model. If you want to integrate against Osmosis, build on it, or run a node, head to Integrate, Build, or Validate once the concepts make sense.

Start with **What is Osmosis?** for the overview, **The OSMO Token** for tokenomics and governance, and the **Glossary** whenever a term is unfamiliar. The **Features** pages explain the mechanisms that make Osmosis distinct (concentrated liquidity, the EIP-1559 fee market, fee abstraction, ProtoRev, and more), and **Getting Started** walks through connecting a wallet and making your first trade.
